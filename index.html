MPC-Chord-Finder

it's a tool to locate chords on the Akai MPCs pads.

Select a single note sample (poly mode) & tune it so that PAD1 is C, hit 16/12LEVELS (tuning), grab a real book and have fun.

You can add chords to the collection by pressing the button "add to collection".
Then try printing the page in landscape mode.
