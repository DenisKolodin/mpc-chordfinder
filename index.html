MPC-Chord-Finder

it's a tool to locate chords on the Akai MPCs pads. It should work with a recent version of chrome.
Set up a chromatic note layout where PAD1 is C on your MPC, grab a real book and have fun.

You can add chords to the collection by pressing the button "add to collection".
Then print the page in landscape mode and turn off your computer ;)
